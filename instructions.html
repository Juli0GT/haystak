How to use haysta:k

In order to get full functionality of the extension, please make all your searches through the search bar in Haysta:K pop-up box.

1. Click on the Haysta:k icon on the top-right corner of your browser.

2. Type your search and click on the "search" button.

3. Google results page will be displayed, look for the right article.

4. Once you have found an article with the right answer for your search, highlight the part of the page that is useful for you. Click again on the Haysta:k icon, add some tags in the tag bar (optional), and click on the save button.

5. Next time you make a search, if the results include one of your saved pages, an Haystack icon will be displayed next to the link for that page.

6. Clicking on HAYSTA:K at the top of the pop-up box will display all the saved pages, including the term used in the search, the url of the page, the highlighted part of it, and the added tags.
